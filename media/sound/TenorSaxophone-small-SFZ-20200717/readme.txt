Tenor Saxophone
---------------

Version 2020-07-17

Made with samples from the Versilian Community Sample Library, a CC0 sample
library created by Versilian Studios LLC. This sound bank is assembled by
roberto@zenvoid.org. Samples have been edited, they have faster attack and
infinite sustain loops.

This sound bank, in several formats and qualities, is available on the
FreePats project web pages:
http://freepats.zenvoid.org/Reed/saxophone.html#TenorSax
